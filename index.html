About Me

Hi, I'm Ethan, an engineer with a passion for building functional websites, robots, and developing games. As a second-year Computer Engineering student at the University of Guelph, I love turning ideas into tangible projects.

My experience spans everything from 3D printed engines to autonomous robots. I enjoy learning new technologies and always pushing myself to find innovative solutions for problems wether that be optimizing PCB layout, mentoring others in competitions, or developing tools to improve life.

I am a member on the Gryphon Racing FSAE team and the UofG Robotics team. On the Gryphon Racing team I work as a member of the Frame subsection where I am currently designing the 3D printed jigs we are going to use to precisely fabricate the frame. On the UofG Robotics team I am on the Electrical subsection where I am currently designing the power distribution board that converts the power from the battery into the different voltages needed for each of the rovers modules. I have a post about the power distribution board that goes into the details of it.
